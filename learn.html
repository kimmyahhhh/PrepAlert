Natural Disasters Preparedness

Preparedness Tips

  • Typhoons: Prepare an emergency kit and know evacuation routes.
  • Earthquakes: Secure heavy furniture and practice safety drills.
  • Volcanic Eruptions: Have masks ready and know safe zones.
  • Flooding: Identify flood-prone areas and create an emergency plan.
  • Landslides: Be aware of warning signs and avoid high-risk areas.
  • Tsunamis: Know evacuation routes and move to higher ground.
  • Droughts: Implement water conservation practices.
